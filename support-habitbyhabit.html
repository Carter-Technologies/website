Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a habit?

When you first open the app, you'll be prompted to create your first habit. Enter the name of your habit, select a duration (7, 14, 21, 30, 60, or 90 days), and you're ready to start tracking!

How do I mark my habit as complete?

Simply tap the checkmark button on the main screen each day when you complete your habit. Your streak will automatically update!

What happens if I miss a day?

If you miss a day, your streak will reset to 0. When you mark your habit as complete the next day, your streak will start at 1 again. Don't worry - every day is a fresh start!

How do I set up daily reminders?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and tap "Reminder Time". Select your preferred time for daily notifications. Make sure you've granted notification permissions when prompted.

I'm not receiving reminder notifications. What should I do?

Make sure you've:

  • Granted notification permissions in iOS Settings > HabitByHabit > Notifications
  • Set a reminder time in the app's Settings
  • Allowed notifications on your device (not in Do Not Disturb mode)
If you're still having issues, try restarting the app or your device.

How do I sync my habits to my calendar?

Calendar sync is a premium feature. If you're a premium user:

  • Go to Settings > Premium Features > Calendar Integration
  • Grant calendar access when prompted
  • Select which calendar to use
  • Tap "Sync All Habits" to sync your habits to your calendar

What are premium features?

Premium features include:

  • Advanced Analytics - Detailed insights into your habit progress
  • Calendar Integration - Sync habits to your iOS Calendar
  • Multiple Habits - Track up to 3 habits simultaneously
  • Streak Reminders - Customizable reminder notifications

How do I view my completion history?

Tap the calendar icon on the main screen to view your completion history. Completed days are shown in green, and you can see your progress over time.

Can I use HabitByHabit without an account?

Yes! HabitByHabit supports anonymous authentication by default. Your data is stored securely, and you can optionally create an account with email/password for additional features.

How do I delete my account or data?

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us through the support email below. We'll process your request within 30 days.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and stored securely in our Supabase database. We never share your data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
