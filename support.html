Frequently Asked Questions

How do I grant Screen Time permission?

When you first start a focus session, SENTINAL will prompt you to grant Screen Time permission. You can also grant it manually in iOS Settings > Screen Time > Family Controls.

How do I select apps to block?

Tap the "Edit" button on the Focus Mode screen, then use the app picker to select which apps you want to block during focus sessions.

Can I exit a focus session early?

Yes! Tap the "Emergency Exit" button during an active session. You'll be asked to confirm before the session ends.

Why aren't my apps being blocked?

Make sure you've:

  • Granted Screen Time permission
  • Selected apps to block using the Edit button
  • Started a focus session

Does SENTINAL collect my data?

No. SENTINAL operates entirely on your device. All data is stored locally and never transmitted to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
