Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

Introduction

SENTINAL ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

SENTINAL operates entirely on your device. All data, including:

  • Selected apps for blocking
  • Focus session settings
  • Timer preferences

...is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. We do not have access to this information.

Permissions

SENTINAL requires the following iOS permissions:

  • Family Controls: Used to block distracting apps during focus sessions. This permission is managed by iOS and requires your explicit consent.
  • Device Activity: Used to monitor and enforce app blocking during active focus sessions.

All permissions are requested with clear explanations and can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not use cloud storage, servers, or any external data services.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Support page.
