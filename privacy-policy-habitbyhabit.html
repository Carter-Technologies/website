Privacy Policy for HabitByHabit

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

Introduction

HabitByHabit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected when you create an account or sign in
  • User ID: Automatically generated unique identifier for your account

Habit Data

  • Habit Name: The name of the habit you're tracking
  • Streak Count: Your current streak of consecutive days
  • Completion History: Dates when you marked your habit as complete
  • Reminder Time: Your preferred daily reminder time
  • Habit Duration: The duration period you set for your habit

Device Information

  • Device Notifications: We request permission to send you daily reminders
  • Calendar Access: We request permission to sync your habits to your calendar (premium feature)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Track your habit progress and streaks
  • Send you daily reminder notifications
  • Sync your habits to your calendar (if enabled)
  • Improve and optimize the App
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase: A secure cloud database service (PostgreSQL)
  • Local Storage: Some data is cached locally on your device for faster access

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Data is stored in secure databases with access controls
  • We use industry-standard security practices

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For data storage and authentication
    • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
    • Data is stored in secure, encrypted databases

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Opt-Out: Disable notifications or calendar sync at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us through our Support page.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted from our systems.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Support page.

Consent

By using our App, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
